Shows total net worth before or after 20% taxes and fee deductions, profit or XIRR by clicking it and chosen interval graph. 

<img alt="Overview widget" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/widgets/overview.webp" />

## Total net worth calculations
All your accounts free money and investment values combined on the current dashboard or all dashboards combined if on the main dashboard.

## Profit calculation
Sums up finalized profits and losses for each day and adds potential profit from current investments

## % change calculation
`profit / (total value - profit)`

## XIRR calculation
Graph passed to 3rd-party XIRR library

<Warning>
    It fails if there are no transactions.
</Warning>
