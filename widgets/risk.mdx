### Risk level
Shows score on the scale of `1-100` how risky your portfolio is.

### Investments size allocation
The ring around the risk score shows how your investments are allocated according to value.
<Accordion title="Critical">
    Extremely high risk of one investment affecting your entire portfolio negatively.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Unbalanced">
    Some investments could affect remarkably your entire portfolio.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Balanced">
    Investments are allocated evenly enough to have low risk on the portfolio.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Almost Even">
    Almost all investments are worth the same amount.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Perfect">
    Perfectly even investment allocation.
</Accordion>

### Timing

Timing calendar shows your investing consistency and timing allocation.

<Accordion title="Very risky">
    Should definately invest more to lower the risk.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Undistributed">
    Spread the invesments out more.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Distributed">
    Very good timing allocation of your investments!
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Perfect">
    Fantastic consistency and timing of your investments. Risk is low thanks to perfect investment distribution in time.
</Accordion>

<Note>
    The darker the square, the higher amount of invesments made during that day.
</Note>
