## Archive a stock

Once the stock becomes inactive and there is no need to show it in the active stocks table, it is possible to click the archive button to hide it away into a separate archive table.
<Note>
  All stocks that are in the archive table are not considered when calculating current risk score.
</Note>

### Remove from archive
To restore a stock and move it back to main stock investments table just click the unarchive button that looks identical to the archive one while expanding the stock in the archive table.
