## Archive a loan

Once the loan becomes inactive and there is no need to show it in the active loans table, it is possible to click the archive button to hide it away into a separate archive table.
<Note>
  All loans that are in the archive table are not considered when calculating current risk score.
</Note>

### Remove from archive
To restore a loan and move it back to main loan investments table just click the unarchive button that looks identical to the archive one while expanding the loan in the archive table.
