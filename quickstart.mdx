## Adding transactions

### From main dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the + icon on the transaction table">
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose transaction name, account, date and amount">
    <Note>
      Amount will be in account currency.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add'">
    <Note>
      Use negative amount for withdrawals.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    <Check>
      New transaction is added and can be found in the transactions table.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### From account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the + icon on the account element">
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose transaction name, date and amount">
    <Note>
      Amount will be in account currency.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Deposit' or withdrawal icon next to it depending on the transaction type">
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    <Check>
      New transaction is added and can be found in the transactions table.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
